What Problems Does GoviShakthi Solve?

Many farmers and agribusinesses struggle due to a lack of market intelligence, inefficient planning, and unpredictable pricing. GoviShakthi simplifies agricultural decision-making with AI-driven insights.

Unstable Market Prices

Without access to real-time market trends, farmers often sell their produce at the wrong time, leading to losses.

Inefficient Crop Planning

Poor planning due to unpredictable demand and climate conditions results in overproduction, waste, or reduced income.

Lack of Real-Time Insights

Limited access to real-time weather, soil, and pest control data leads to lower productivity and higher risks.
